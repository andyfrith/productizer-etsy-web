Constitution / Mission

Mission

Help Etsy sellers turn design ideas into listing-ready POD products—starting with concepts and mocks, not market research.

Not in scope yet

Now: Design concept studio — create and store concepts, branch variations, composite onto product mocks, approve assets. Campaign labels are light metadata only until the research module exists.

Vision

Productizer is a product creation platform for niche-oriented print-on-demand and (later) digital listings on Etsy. Sellers move from a design concept to approved mocks and exportable assets in one workspace—with AI-assisted variation where it helps.

Principles

  • Clarity — readable UI and docs; real thumbnails in the app, not abstract placeholders in specs
  • Polished UX — shadcn/ui, thoughtful motion, loading and empty states
  • Concept lineage — every variation and mock traces back to a concept
  • One product type per phase — poster → sign → tumbler → shirt incrementally
  • Local-first — Postgres via Docker Compose; production hosting undecided
  • Tested flows — Vitest for logic; Playwright for critical paths

Stack detail: tech-stack.html

Success (initial phases)

  • Create a concept, generate or upload variations, approve one, see it on a poster mock—without leaving Productizer.
  • Assets stored locally with thumb, card, and full renditions.
  • Time from new concept → first approved mock under 30 minutes (manual or AI path).
