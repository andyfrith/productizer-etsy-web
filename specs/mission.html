Mission

Help Etsy sellers turn design ideas into listing-ready POD products—starting with concepts, not market research.

Current focus (initial phases): Design concept creation & management—the Image Generator module: create, store, branch variations, composite onto product mocks, approve assets. Niche is a light label on concepts for now, not a research platform.

Vision

Productizer is a product creation platform for niche-oriented print-on-demand and (later) digital listings on Etsy. Sellers move from a design concept to approved mocks and exportable assets in one workspace—with AI-assisted variation where it helps.

Principles

  • Visual-first — lists, galleries, exports use mini mockups and thumbnails.
  • Polished UX — shadcn/ui, thoughtful motion (Framer Motion), loading/empty/error states; studio-grade feel throughout.
  • Concept lineage — every variation and mock traces back to a concept.
  • One product type per phase — add poster → sign → tumbler → shirt incrementally.
  • Local-first via Docker Compose — Postgres in containers, Next.js on host; production hosting undecided.
  • Tested flows — Vitest for logic; Playwright E2E for critical concept → mock paths.

Stack detail: tech-stack.html

Success (initial phases)

  • Create a concept, generate or upload variations, approve one, see it on a poster mock—without leaving Productizer.
  • All assets stored locally with thumb/card/full renditions for UI and export preview.
  • Time from new concept → first approved mock < 30 minutes (manual or AI path).
